Newspapers are a huge source of information which is read by most people. However It contains information in an unstructured form. If this information is processed and analyzed properly it can serve as a great asset.

The objective of our project is to create a prototype of a system that can classify news into crime and non-crime. We provide classification of real time news from online newspaper articles. The news is crawled from various news sites and is classified into crime and non-crime.However the classification of news is done using Machine learning approach. Three different classifiers are used to classify the news and a report of the predicted values is generated. The system uses the concepts of Natural Language Processing to process the words in the news and to make it fit for classification.

The system will be beneficial for anyone who is interested to get an idea of the number of crimes occurring. Also it gives the system produces quick results rather than the manual classification techniques.

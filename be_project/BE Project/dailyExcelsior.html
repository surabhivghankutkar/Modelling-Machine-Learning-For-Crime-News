The Daily Excelsior is an English-language newspaper published in Jammu, a city in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. It was established by S. D. Rohmetra. The newspaper has been in publication since 1 January 1965. Its current editors are Kamal Rohmetra and Neeraj Rohmetra. Daily Excelsior is leading newspaper of Jammu region.
